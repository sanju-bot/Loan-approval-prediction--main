## 🔑 Key Factors for Approval

**What helps:**
- ✅ High annual income
- ✅ High CIBIL score (750+)
- ✅ Low loan amount relative to income
- ✅ Few dependents
- ✅ Graduate education
- ✅ Stable employment

**What hurts:**
- ❌ Low income
- ❌ Low CIBIL score
- ❌ High loan amount
- ❌ Many dependents
- ❌ Low assets
- ❌ Self-employed
